Get to Know Our Program

We are a team of students from the Bachelor of Industrial Technology, majoring in Foods, at NEUST CIT. our studies focus on food technology, modern processing techniques, and safety standards. We are committed to advancing sustainable methods in food production, combining creativity with technical knowledge to positively impact the food industry.
